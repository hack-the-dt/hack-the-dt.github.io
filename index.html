Alpine Coding Competitions (ACC)

Developed as a friendly yet ambitious contest, the Alpine Coding Competition brings together students and researchers from the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano and the University of Innsbruck. Set against the backdrop of the Alps, the event blends collaboration and competition: teams tackle real-world coding challenges, showcase their technical skills, and exchange fresh ideas. The competition fosters cross-border connections, encourages creative problem solving, and highlights the talent growing in our region.

MATISSE challenge

MATISSE (Model-based engineering of Digital Twins for early verification and validation of Industrial Systems) is a European research project developing a cloud-native framework to automate the engineering, federation and continuous validation of Digital Twins for industrial systems. MATISSE comprises a consortium of over 30 partners from 7 countries and combines model-based and data-driven techniques with cloud services.

Find out more at matisse-kdt.eu.

Hackathon Challenge: GenAI for Digital Twin Modeling

In this challenge, participants explore how generative AI can support the creation of Digital Twin models. You will experiment with GenAI-based assistants to:

Generate and refine system models,
Validate model consistency and completeness,
Explore agentic approaches to connect natural language and models,
Demonstrate how GenAI accelerates the engineering of Digital Twins.
